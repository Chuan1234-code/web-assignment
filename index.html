A Journey Beyond
Limits

Eileen Gu grew up in San Francisco, started skiing at age 3, and began competing internationally at 15. At 18, she won three medals at the Beijing Winter Olympics representing China — a decision that made her one of the most talked-about athletes in the world.

Since then, she has walked the Louis Vuitton runway, appeared on the covers of Time and Vogue, enrolled at Stanford, and spent a term at Oxford. She has never treated these as separate lives — to her, they are all the same thing.

Olympic Champion · Freestyle Skiing

Sports February 2022 — Beijing Winter Olympics

Defying Gravity, Defining a Generation

On the slopes of Zhangjiakou, Eileen Gu rewrote what was thought possible in freestyle skiing. Her signature move — a double cork 1440 she invented and landed for the first time in competition — stunned judges, rivals, and spectators alike. She became the first freeskier in history to win three medals at a single Winter Olympics.

But her athletic legacy stretches far beyond one Games. She holds the most X-Games gold medals of any female freeskier, and has consistently pushed the technical ceiling of a sport built on pure human flight.

  • Beijing 2022 — 2 Gold, 1 Silver
  • Milano Cortina 2026 — 1 Gold, 2 Silver
  • Multiple FIS Freestyle Skiing World Cup victories
  • Record-breaking X-Games medal haul in Big Air & Halfpipe
  • First athlete to land double cork 1440 in women's competition

Fashion Icon · Global Brand Ambassador

Fashion 2021–Present — Global Campaigns

The New Face of Luxury

Before the 2022 Olympics, the fashion industry had already taken notice of Eileen Gu. She signed with Louis Vuitton, appeared in campaigns for Tiffany & Co. and Estée Lauder, and walked runways in Paris, Milan, and Shanghai — all before turning 19.

What sets her apart is the credibility she brings to both worlds. She is not an athlete doing fashion on the side, nor a model with a sporting background. She competes at the highest level and works with the biggest houses, and neither seems to compromise the other.

  • Louis Vuitton Global Brand Ambassador
  • Tiffany & Co. Ambassador — campaign shot in New York & Beijing
  • Vogue China, Vogue Hong Kong, and international cover star
  • Estée Lauder, Anta, Beats by Dre, Red Bull partnerships

Stanford University · Quantum Physics

Academics 2022 — Stanford University

Mind as Sharp as the Mountain's Edge

Eileen Gu has always insisted that being an academic is an integral part of who she is. She came to Stanford after the 2022 Beijing Olympics with a 1580 SAT score, having already become the first student in her school's history to graduate a year early. At Stanford, she dove into quantum physics, joined a sorority, and settled on an international relations major — all while competing at the sport's highest level.

In the fall of 2024, she extended her academic journey to Oxford as an exchange student, studying creative nonfiction and entrepreneurship. As she told Oxford's student paper Cherwell: "being an academic is an integral part of who I am, so that's why I've remained a full-time student throughout my life." She is, in her own words, a self-described nerd.

  • Took Quantum Physics at Stanford
  • SAT Score: 1580/1600
  • Advocate for STEM education access for young women
